Dr. Crow

XNA C# Coded With Hand Made Pixel Art

 

Overview

This game was coded completely in C# XNA. All the sprite art was hand made in photoshop. This scrolling parallax title screen switches from night to day.

Gameplay

The object of the game is to kill as many zombies as possible and see how long you can survive. Killing zombies will earn you XP to level up. When you level up your health is restored to full. Zombies strength increases the longer you survive and stronger zombies spawn during the night.

Sprite Art

All sprite art was created by hand in photoshop.
