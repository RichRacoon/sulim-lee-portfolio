BuzzCode App

 

Overview

This is BuzzCode. BuzzCode is an app that allows users to order alcohol delivery or deliver alcohol to other users for money. It works similar to Uber eats, leveraging your device's location to pair you up with the closest user who is delivering or ordering.

Theme and Typography

Here is the details for the theme I created for BuzzCode. This theme is more busy than my other apps but still utilizes the philosophies of UX to provide users with the best experience possible.

Orders

BuzzCode provides a rubust interface for both the buyer and the seller. Workflows for placing orders and making deliveries are streamlined and made as simple as possible.
