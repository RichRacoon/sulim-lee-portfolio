Abacus App

 

Overview

This is my Abacus App. This is a minimalist design app designed to teach people how to do math using an abacus.

Mr. Bean

I created Mr. Bean to help new users through the tutorial and teach them how to use an abacus. Correct and efficient use of the abacus makes Mr. Bean very happy.

Courses

The app guides users through progressively difficult courses and assignments in addition, subtraction, multiplication and division. As users finish assignments, more difficult assignments will be unlocked on the course screens.

Results

User's results are tracked on the results screen as well as their best times for each assignment.
