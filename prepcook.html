Prep Cook App

 

Overview

This is my Prepcook App. This app is designed to help prep cooks and line cooks streamline their daily list preparation to increase efficiency of the employees and reduce labor costs for the employers.

Colors, Theme and Typography

Here is all the information the theme I created for the app.

Interface

This app was designed with minimalist UX design philosophies. After logging in, to display the menu, users must hit the hamburger menu button in the top corner. This will slide out the side nav for users to navigate around the app. From here they can adjust settings or begin building their list.

Tabs

The kitchen areas are divided into tabs so that users can quickly count all the items in a single area before moving on to the next.

Counts

When the user has selected a day and an area, they are taken to the count screen. Here a complete list of all the items found in that area are displayed. Users count these items, then the app adds up all counted items of the same type. The app compares the amount counted with the amount the store will need for that particular day. It then generates a list of all the items that the user needs to prepare.
